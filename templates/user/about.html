This is going to be an event management website that helps people finding events easily and effectively. This website is going to serve as a middle link between the Event managing companies and the workers working in the event. This website contains almost every type of events.

Our Vision

Our visions is to connect the event managing companies and Workhands. Increase employment in the country. Make every event succesfull, effortless, memorable and our main goal is make the event best in every way. It will also help the workers getting full payment of the job they are doing by removing the middle people eating commission.
